TOO MANY DISEASE CASES

Since 1980, the number of disease outbreaks per year has more than tripled. This is because the conventional method for dealing with outbreaks is reaction, not prevention.

REDUCE DISEASE OUTBREAKS

By preventing and reacting we will reduce the number of disease cases and therefore, outbreaks. This helps create the more sustainable future that we all strive for.

PREDICTION AT IT'S FINEST

We will be able to make our mission happen with the help of artificial intelligence. It's real-time predictions will let us know about parasitic migration, meaning that we can prevent, not just react.

OUR SOLUTION

We are automating data collection and analyzing said data to predict most at-risk populations. Our predictions will be displayed on a user-friendly map for governments and pharmaceuticals to view and know where to deploy targeted aid.

Better Prevention

With our technology our customers will be able to warn people about rises in mosquito population. This will help the people in at-risk areas better prepare with mosquito nets and other repelling techniques.

Quicker Reaction

Our customers will also be able to go through their normal routine for an epidemic/outbreak as it's happening, and not a couple weeks after. This will save millions of lives that are normally ended because of slow reaction.

Reduced Resource Waste

Since our customers will know exactly where the biggest need for resources is, they can reduce the waste by only sending resources to those areas.
